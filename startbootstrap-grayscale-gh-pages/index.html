What We Do

Probxorsol Inc. is a software development and training firm. We are a Disabled Veteran Owned Firm that specializes in database solutions, COTS implementation, web application development, testing, and full scope project management. We also engage in specialized training in computer programming in a variety of languages and administrative training in a variety of software packages.

Specialization

We specialize in Web based systems support, customization or replacement of extraneous tech systems, development of high quality desktop/web based tools to improve performance and data handling, Information Assurance Audits, Use Case Testing, Project Management, Business Analysis, Data Analysis, Penetration Testing, and Cyber Security Analysis of small to Enterprise level systems.

Methodology

We use the SAFE Scaled Agile Framework in our management methodology Please refer to the SAFE website for further information. We utilize PMBOK principles in the overall design of our implementations.
